# DevWeb3Jogja: Before & After Blitz

## What is this?

DevWeb3Jogja is a national builder hub. Developers come in from across Indonesia, not just Jogja. The Blitz itself is the hackathon for Web3 projects, with AI development pushed hard. The Before and After programs exist so the work that comes out of the Blitz keeps going instead of dying the week after.

Two tracks, three sessions each.

## Before Blitz

1. Preparation day. Onboard developers who aren't already in the study group. Walk them through the Monad ecosystem and MonSkills, and get everyone to a first testnet deploy so nobody turns up to the Blitz cold.

2. Ideation. Find ideas that actually fit Monad. Real research time here, looking for protocols that don't exist yet so teams build on top of the ecosystem instead of rebuilding what's already there.

3. Advanced session. Take the ideas and build a working app, then close with a live demo. Teams should walk into the Blitz with something already running.

## After Blitz

This is also where every founder gets connected to Monad directly, for consultation and next steps.

1. Post-Blitz review. Go through what each team built. Won? Find the gap and push it further. Lost? Find what's fixable and fix it.

2. Compounding cycle. Run the sprint again, one protocol in 24 hours, so the momentum doesn't stall.

3. Builder meetup. Swap the tools teams actually used, give Delta V a proper intro, and go through what's coming next from Monad.

## Timeline (rough)

About 3 weeks of runway before the Blitz, run weekly or bi-weekly async so it doesn't eat everyone's schedule. Then 1 to 2 weeks of active follow-up after.

Blitz Jogja lands early to mid September, after CoinFest Asia (August 20 to 21). No exact date locked with Monad yet. We confirm that once the venue and contract are set.
